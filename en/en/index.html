Frequently asked

Good to know

What is your minimum order quantity?

We produce from a single piece to a recurring series. Volume pricing applies from the first bulk order – just tell us your desired quantity.

How much does embroidery cost?

The price depends on the design, stitch count, textile and quantity. You receive a transparent quote with volume pricing, usually within one working day.

Can you embroider our company logo?

Yes. We digitise your logo into an embroidery file and embroider textiles precisely and durably – optionally with neutral (white-label) delivery to your customers.

Do you offer framework contracts and recurring deliveries?

Yes. For recurring orders we agree fixed terms and predictable delivery dates – with consistent quality from batch to batch.

Which materials do you use?

By default 100% GOTS-certified organic cotton from Stanley/Stella – toxin-tested and skin-friendly. Other textiles are available on request.
