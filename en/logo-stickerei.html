From file to finished series

We review your logo, digitise it into an embroidery file (punching) and agree size, position and thread colours with you. Before the run you receive a sample for approval.

That way your logo looks identical on every piece – from the first to the thousandth order.

  • Professional logo digitising (embroidery file)
  • Sample before the run for approval
  • Up to 15 thread colours, Madeira premium thread
  • Shirts, polos, caps, bags, workwear & more
  • Neutral B2B delivery (white-label) available

Which files do I need?

Ideally a vector logo (AI, EPS, PDF, SVG). We also work with good PNG/JPG artwork.

How big will the logo be?

Chest logos are usually 7–10 cm, caps smaller. We recommend the right size per textile and level of detail.

Logo embroidery – FAQ

How much does embroidering a logo cost?

The price depends on stitch count, textile and quantity. You receive a transparent quote with volume pricing, usually within one working day.

Is there a setup fee?

A small one-off digitising fee may apply – it is waived for follow-up orders using the same file.
