Certified, not greenwashing

By default we work with 100% GOTS-certified organic cotton from Stanley/Stella – ecologically and socially audited along the supply chain. Our Madeira embroidery threads are OEKO-TEX®-certified.

That turns promotional textiles and gifts into sustainable brand ambassadors that fit a credible presence.

  • 100% GOTS-certified organic cotton (Stanley/Stella)
  • OEKO-TEX®-tested Madeira embroidery threads
  • Production in Karlsruhe – short distances
  • Durable embroidery instead of fast-wearing print
  • Neutral, resource-friendly packaging on request

Why organic cotton?

GOTS excludes harmful substances, conserves water and soil and secures social standards – verifiably, not just as a promise.

Sustainability – FAQ

What does GOTS mean?

The Global Organic Textile Standard is the leading label for textiles made from organically grown natural fibres – with ecological and social criteria across the entire supply chain.

Are the threads sustainable too?

We use OEKO-TEX®-certified embroidery threads from Madeira – toxin-tested and durable.
